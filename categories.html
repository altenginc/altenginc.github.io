About Us

  • AltEng Inc is a boutique consulting and engineering firm started as a small minority owned business. The firm specializes in developing Alternative Energy and Decarbonization solutions for the Railway Industry. Our and commercial specialists, with over 35 years of experience, offer customized consulting services & solutions to meet Business and Government Agency specific needs for a wide range of industries. ​
  • Our principals have extensive experience in design, and development of Rail Transportation equipment as well as application of non-carbon technologies like battery and hydrogen technologies and are in a unique position to recommend the most appropriate options to clients. We are currently providing specialized knowledge to development of mainline battery-powered rail equipment for some major customers.
  • AltEng Inc. possesses the expertise and capability to assess the technical and economic viability of acquiring rail transportation equipment including developing business cases and developing specifications and tender documents.
  • AltEng Inc.’s principals have executed projects several for North American and International railroads and are intimately familiar with global rail operating and maintenance practices and standards.​
